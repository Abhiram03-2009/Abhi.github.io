NBA Teams



Eastern Conference Western Conference

Each team in The NBA represents a city from a state in the U.S.A. Games usually occur 3-4 times a week. Although some states’ cities don’t have a team, 30 of the consecutive 50 states have a team. There are also some teams that are from the same state, but just different cities. For example, the Los Angeles Lakers are from California. The Sacramento Kings are also from California. The Los Angeles Clippers are as well a part of California. There are three teams just for California as shown above. Texas also has the Dallas Mavericks and the San Antonio Spurs. Many states don’t have 2 or more teams, but the states who do have, spread the fans into the required number of teams into groups. They might like one team in their state and not the other, since there is more than one team in the state.
